B.Tech CSE (Data Science)-4-Year Curriculum


    Year 1 - Foundation (Basic of programing, Maths & Engineering)


  1. Semester 1

    • Engineering Mathematics I (Calculus and Linear Algebra)
    • Engineering Physics
    • Fundamentals of Electrical & Electronics Engineering
    • Problem Solving using C++ & Python

  2. Semester 2

    1. Engineering Mathematics II (Differential Equations & Statistics)
    2. Object-Oriented Programming (C++ / Java)
    3. Basic Mechanical / Civil Engineering
    4. Digital Logic Design

Year 2 - Core CS & Mathematical Foundations for Data Science

Semester 3

  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Computer Organization and Architecture
  • Database Management Systems
  • Probability & Statistics

Semester 4

  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Data Mining and Warehousing
  • Linear Algebra for Data Science

    Year 3 - Data Science Core + AI + Big Data

  • Semester 5

    1. Machine Learning
    2. Big Data Analytics (Hadoop / Spark)
    3. Deep Learning Fundamentals
    4. Data Visualization

  • Semester 6

    • Cloud Computing
    • Data Security and Privacy
    • Reinforcement Learning / Advanced ML
    • Software Engineering

Year 4 - Advanced Topics, Research & Project Work

Semester 7

  1. Artificial Neural Networks and Applications
  2. Data Science for Business Intelligence / Decision Making
